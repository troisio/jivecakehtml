Frequently Asked Questions

How much does this service cost?

$10 per month for each event which you make publicly availible.

Does JiveCake charge any other fees?

JiveCake does not charge any other fees. Your payment provider may charge fees for transactions, charges or refunds. When using Paypal for accepting payments, refer to Paypal's fee policy. When using Stripe for accepting payments, refer to Stripe pricing policy.

What fees are associated with refunds?

Your payment processer may impose a fee for a refund. When using Paypal for accepting payments, refer to Paypal's fee policy. When using Stripe for accepting payments, refer to Stripe's fee policy.

If I make changes to transactions in my Paypal or Stripe account, do they reflect in my JiveCake account?

No. Only changes made through JiveCake will be reflected.
